Country Hangman



TIP: If the page has a lot of empty space, zoom in!

How To Play

  • You have to guess the mystery country, generated randomly.
  • The blanks(_) at the top signify the number of letters in the country's name.
  • If the country has more than one word, the words are separated by a slash(/).
  • If you think a particular letter is in the answer, click on it, or press the button on the keyboard.
  • If it is indeed in the answer, then the position of the letter amongst the other letters and blanks will be shown at the top.
  • If, however, that letter is not in the answer, then the picture of the hangman will gain a body part.
  • If the picture of the hangman is completed, which will be after seven incorrect guesses, then you lose.

Important: Keep in Mind

  • All the 193 UN member countries + TAIWAN, PALESTINE and VATICAN CITY are included.
  • The ENGLISH name for all the countries has been used. Eg - Türkiye is written as TURKEY, Côte d'Ivoire is written as IVORY COAST, Cabo Verde is written as CAPE VERDE, and Timor-Leste is written as EAST TIMOR. Any accent marks or symbols atop letters that are not in English are excluded.
  • The most used names of the countries are used, not the full name. Eg - United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland is written as UNITED KINGDOM.
  • All 'THE's at the beginning of country names have been omitted.
  • Country abbreviations are not used. It is not USA, it is UNITED STATES OF AMERICA.
  • Since there are two Congos, the official names of both have been used: REPUBLIC OF THE CONGO and DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF THE CONGO.
  • The two Koreas are written as NORTH KOREA and SOUTH KOREA.
